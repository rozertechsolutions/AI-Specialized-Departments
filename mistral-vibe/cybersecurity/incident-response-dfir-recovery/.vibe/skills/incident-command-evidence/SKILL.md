# Incident Command Evidence

Mission/scope: static governance and evidence planning. Inputs: report, criteria, scope, evidence references, authority constraints. Preconditions: no live access; owners by role. Output: readiness, declaration support, role matrix, logs, evidence plan, manifest, custody. Permissions: read-only. Dependencies: command, legal, privacy, technical, business, safety, evidence owners. Stop: live action, legal conclusion, external communication, evidence alteration. Completion: provenance, confidence, limitations, human decisions, approval state, review need. Human review and prohibited actions follow AGENTS.md.
