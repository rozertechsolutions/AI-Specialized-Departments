# Risk, Exceptions, and Remediation

Confirm scope, assets, business context, approved risk method, evidence, owner, deadlines, approver, exception duration, compensating controls, and closure criteria. Produce risk statements, risk assessments, risk register entries, treatment plans, exception records, finding records, remediation plans, and closure validations.

Use `cyber-risk-exceptions-agent` for risk and exceptions. Use `assurance-evidence-remediation-agent` for remediation and closure evidence. Route high-impact closure or acceptance packages to `independent-assurance-reviewer`.

Stop if asked to accept risk, grant or extend exceptions, close risk or findings automatically, suppress residual risk, downgrade severity without approved rationale, or change production systems.
