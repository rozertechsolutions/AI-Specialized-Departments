# Governance, Policy, and Frameworks

Confirm authorized scope, owner, source versions, evidence, reviewer, approver, and decision needed. Produce governance charters, responsibility matrices, policy lifecycle records, control entries, requirement mappings, gap records, remediation plans, or change-impact assessments.

Use `governance-policy-frameworks-agent` for ownership. Distinguish mandatory requirements from recommendations; mark exact, partial, inherited, compensating, non-applicable, and unmapped relationships. Record assumptions, limitations, confidence, and human review needs.

Stop if asked to approve strategy, publish or enforce policy, provide final legal interpretation, claim compliance/certification, or modify technical controls.
