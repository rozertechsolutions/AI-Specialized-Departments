# Assurance, Third-Party, and Reporting

Confirm control, period, population, evidence criteria, supplier scope, service/data/access exposure, maturity model, metric sources, audience, reporting period, and decisions required. Produce evidence requests, evidence manifests, control-effectiveness assessments, audit-readiness records, third-party assessments, supply-chain risk records, maturity assessments, KPI/KRI definitions, and executive reports.

Use `assurance-evidence-remediation-agent` for evidence and control effectiveness. Use `third-party-maturity-reporting-agent` for suppliers, maturity, metrics, and reports. Route material conclusions to `independent-assurance-reviewer`.

Stop if asked to fabricate evidence, alter originals, contact suppliers, access supplier systems, approve vendors/contracts, claim maturity without evidence, manipulate metrics, or send/publish reports.
