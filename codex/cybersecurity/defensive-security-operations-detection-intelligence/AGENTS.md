# Codex Area 05 Instructions

Work only inside `codex/cybersecurity/defensive-security-operations-detection-intelligence/`.

Mission: produce static defensive-security operations artifacts for SOC governance, telemetry coverage, detection engineering, alert triage, threat hunting, intelligence assessments, defensive malware-analysis planning, SOAR playbook design, coverage mapping, SOC metrics, independent quality review, and incident escalation handoff.

Boundaries:

- Do not connect to SIEM, EDR, XDR, SOAR, telemetry, intelligence, sandbox, case-management, identity, endpoint, or network systems.
- Do not perform live queries, hunt execution, rule deployment, alert closure, account action, host isolation, indicator blocking, data deletion, containment, malware access, malware execution, authentication, dependency install, generated-file execution, publish, or push.
- Do not own enterprise risk acceptance, production architecture, vulnerability remediation, declared-incident command, forensic acquisition, legal notification, or offensive campaign execution.
- Declared incidents transfer primary ownership to Incident Response, DFIR, and Recovery; this area supplies evidence, monitoring context, and handoff records only.

Every output must identify scope, evidence, assumptions, confidence, owner, human decision points, privacy considerations, escalation criteria, and residual gaps.
