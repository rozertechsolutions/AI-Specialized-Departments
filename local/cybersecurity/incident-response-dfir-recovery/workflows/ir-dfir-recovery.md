# IR DFIR Recovery Workflows

Readiness; triage and declaration support; active coordination by plan; evidence preservation and forensic acquisition planning; containment and eradication option analysis; secure recovery planning; ransomware/destructive coordination; data exposure/privacy coordination; tabletop exercise; post-incident review.

Each workflow must include static inputs, scope, evidence, assumptions, decision owner, independent reviewer, stop conditions, failure behavior, and completion criteria.
