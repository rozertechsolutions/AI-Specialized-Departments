# Responsibility Model

The area owns declared-incident coordination support and assurance, not routine SOC monitoring, enterprise legal authority, public communications, regulator notification, business-continuity ownership, production administration, or offensive testing.

Mandatory domains:

1. Preparedness and governance.
2. Classification, scoping, and command support.
3. Evidence preservation and chain of custody.
4. Digital forensics planning and analysis coordination.
5. Containment and eradication planning.
6. Recovery and service restoration assurance.
7. Specialized scenario coordination.
8. Crisis, legal, privacy, regulatory, and communications handoff.
9. Post-incident review and corrective action.
10. Incident assurance and independent review.

Independence rule: any critical conclusion, recovery claim, closure claim, or residual-risk note requires review by an owner who did not create the artifact.
