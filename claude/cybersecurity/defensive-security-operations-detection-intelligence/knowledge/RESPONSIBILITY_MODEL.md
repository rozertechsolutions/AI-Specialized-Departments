# Responsibility Model

- SOC governance: service scope, roles, escalation, handoff, quality, evidence, case states, exception paths, and human authority.
- Telemetry coverage: source purpose, events, fields, timestamps, identities, context, integrity, quality, privacy, and retention dependencies.
- Detection engineering: hypotheses, logic, context, severity, triage, tuning, validation, ownership, versioning, and retirement.
- Alert triage: evidence preservation, enrichment needs, confidence, scope, impact, outcomes, rationale, and escalation.
- Threat hunting: hypotheses, analytical plan, pivots, limitations, evidence handling, and handoff criteria.
- Threat intelligence: requirements, source evaluation, confidence, relevance, assessments, dissemination, feedback, and expiry.
- Malware-analysis planning: safe intake, classification, isolated analysis plan, evidence, indicators, and escalation without sample handling.
- SOAR design: playbooks with disabled actions, explicit human gates, idempotency, rollback, auditability, and failure behavior.
- Coverage mapping: threats, techniques, assets, telemetry, detections, controls, gaps, and validation evidence.
- Independent review: assurance for detections, triage, hunts, intelligence, automation, metrics, weak evidence, bias, and misleading metrics.
