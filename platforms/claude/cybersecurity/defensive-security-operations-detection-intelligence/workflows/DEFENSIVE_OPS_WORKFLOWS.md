# Defensive Operations Workflows

## SOC and Telemetry

Confirm mission, scope, assets, services, stakeholders, privacy constraints, legal constraints, and incident handoff. Define roles, ownership, escalation, case states, evidence, quality, telemetry dependencies, measurable service criteria, and human authority without connecting systems.

## Detection, Triage, Hunting, and Intelligence

Design detections from hypotheses, telemetry, logic, evidence, triage, tuning, validation, ownership, and retirement criteria. Triage supplied alerts by preserving source, timestamps, entities, evidence, rule version, scope, rationale, and outcome. Plan hunts and produce intelligence assessments without live execution or feed connections.

## Malware Planning, SOAR, Coverage, and Quality

Design malware-analysis plans without sample access. Design SOAR playbooks with external actions disabled and explicit human approval. Assess coverage with validation evidence only. Review SOC quality without publishing automatically.
