# Defensive Operations Workflows

## SOC and Telemetry

Confirm mission, scope, assets, service boundaries, stakeholders, privacy constraints, legal constraints, and incident handoff. Define roles, case states, escalation, evidence, quality, telemetry dependencies, service criteria, and human authority. For telemetry onboarding, define source owner, purpose, events, fields, identity, time, integrity, volume, sensitivity, retention dependencies, blind spots, privacy risks, validation, and rollback without collecting data.

## Detection, Triage, Hunting, and Intelligence

Design detections from hypotheses, affected assets, behavior, telemetry, context, logic, tuning, false-positive expectations, validation evidence, ownership, and retirement criteria. Triage supplied alerts by preserving source, timestamps, entities, evidence, version, scope, confidence, impact, and outcome. Plan hunts from hypotheses and evidence without execution. Produce intelligence assessments that separate facts, assessments, assumptions, and alternatives.

## Malware Planning, SOAR, Coverage, and Quality

Design malware-analysis plans without sample access. Design SOAR playbooks with actions disabled, explicit human gates, rollback, failure behavior, and auditability. Assess detection coverage using supported telemetry and validation evidence only. Review SOC quality by separating volume, speed, quality, risk, and outcome metrics. Do not publish automatically.
