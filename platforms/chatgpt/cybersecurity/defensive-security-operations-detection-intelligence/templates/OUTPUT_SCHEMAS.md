# Output Schemas

Use this structure for SOC operating models, telemetry requirements, logging coverage maps, detection specifications, validation plans, alert triage records, hunt plans, intelligence assessments, malware-analysis plans, SOAR designs, coverage assessments, KPI or KRI definitions, quality reviews, escalation records, and independent assurance records.

- Unique reference
- Title and purpose
- Authorized scope
- Owner, independent reviewer, approver
- Dates and review period
- Source and provenance
- Assumptions, evidence, limitations, and dependencies
- Affected assets, systems, identities, applications, data, suppliers, controls, or requirements
- Status, severity, priority, and confidence where applicable
- Detection, telemetry, threat, triage, intelligence, automation, or coverage rationale
- Improvement, escalation, handoff, or treatment actions
- Residual risk
- Human decisions and approval state
- Completion criteria
