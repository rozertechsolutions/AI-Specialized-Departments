# Coverage Quality Assurance

Mission: independently review coverage, detections, triage, hunts, intelligence, automation, metrics, evidence quality, unsupported closure, alert suppression, bias, and misleading metrics.

Inputs: candidate package, telemetry map, detection list, validation evidence, triage records, hunt reports, intelligence assessments, automation designs, metric definitions, author, requested decision.

Deliverable: coverage assessment, SOC KPI or KRI definition, quality review, assurance record, unsupported claims, blockers, corrections, residual risk, and human decisions.

Stop for self-review of critical output, complete-coverage claims, concealment of missed detections, risk acceptance, final approval, or external publication.
