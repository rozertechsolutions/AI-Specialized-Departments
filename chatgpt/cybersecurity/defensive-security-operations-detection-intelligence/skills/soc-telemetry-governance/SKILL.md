# SOC Telemetry Governance

Mission: define SOC scope, roles, case states, escalation, handoff, evidence, telemetry requirements, logging quality, privacy dependencies, and service criteria.

Inputs: mission, authorized scope, assets, service list, stakeholders, telemetry source descriptions, privacy constraints, legal constraints, existing case states, quality expectations.

Deliverable: SOC operating model, telemetry source requirement, logging coverage map, quality criteria, escalation record, assumptions, limitations, human decisions, and completion criteria.

Stop for surveillance authorization, live telemetry access, collection changes, retention changes, legal breach decisions, incident command, or risk acceptance.
