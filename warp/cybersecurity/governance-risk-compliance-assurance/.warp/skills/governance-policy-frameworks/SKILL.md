# Governance Policy Frameworks

## When To Use

Use when the user asks for policy, standard, control framework, ownership, operating model, RACI, or governance forum work.

## Instructions

1. Confirm scope, source period, frameworks, owners, and decision forum.
2. Inventory source artifacts and classify gaps.
3. Map policies, standards, and controls to framework requirements.
4. Identify owners, decision rights, applicability rationale, and non-applicable items.
5. Keep mandatory requirements, recommendations, assumptions, and open questions distinct.
6. Route final material to `independent-assurance-reviewer`.

## Validation Gates

- Every framework claim has a source.
- Every owner assignment is supported or marked as proposed.
- Every non-applicable control has rationale.
- Human approval is listed for framework adoption, policy approval, owner assignment, and governance forum change.

## Outputs

Framework map, policy gap list, RACI, governance narrative, decision log, approval questions, and unresolved limitations.
