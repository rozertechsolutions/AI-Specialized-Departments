# Independent Assurance Review

## When To Use

Use before material is presented as complete for governance approval, risk acceptance, exception approval, audit support, supplier review, maturity reporting, or committee reporting.

## Instructions

1. Confirm independence and identify the draft owner.
2. Verify scope and source set.
3. Trace each material claim to source support.
4. Test rating consistency, owner fields, date fields, approval path, and limitation disclosure.
5. Classify findings as blockers, required corrections, advisory improvements, or residual limitations.
6. Return findings without editing reviewed material.

## Validation Gates

- Every material claim is traceable.
- Every rating has criteria.
- Every approval dependency is explicit.
- Unresolved assumptions are visible.
- Any independence conflict stops the review.

## Outputs

Review findings, severity, required corrections, advisory improvements, residual limitations, and final readiness statement.
