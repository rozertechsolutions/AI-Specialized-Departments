# Testing Findings PSIRT Remediation

Define proportionate SAST, DAST, SCA, IaC, container, API, mobile, and manual-review strategy without execution. Preserve supplied finding source, scanner, version, scope, and evidence. Normalize duplicates and classify confirmed, probable, not reproduced, false positive, or needs validation. Support product vulnerability intake, validation planning, severity, affected versions, customer impact, remediation, retest, disclosure dependencies, and human approvals. Do not run scanners, access live targets, contact reporters automatically, disclose, or close critical findings through self-review.
