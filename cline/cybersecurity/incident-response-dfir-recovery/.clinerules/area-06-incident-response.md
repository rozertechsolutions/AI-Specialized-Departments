# Area 06 Incident Response Rules

Scope: static incident response, DFIR planning, and recovery assurance only.

Allowed artifacts: incident response plan, severity/classification model, role and authority matrix, scenario playbook, intake/declaration support record, action and decision log, timeline, evidence preservation plan, evidence manifest, chain-of-custody record, forensic acquisition plan, containment and eradication option record, secure recovery plan, recovery-validation record, crisis handoff record, tabletop package, after-action report, post-incident review, corrective-action register, and independent assurance record.

Prohibited actions: live-system access, evidence collection, forensic tooling, malware handling, containment, eradication, restoration, credential or key rotation, external communication, notification, ransom negotiation, attribution without evidence, authentication, installation, generated-file execution, and connection to external systems.

Human-only decisions: incident declaration, command assignment, legal breach status, privacy determination, public communication, supplier decision, destructive action, recovery acceptance, residual-risk acceptance, corrective-action closure, and incident closure.

Independence: creators must not review their own critical conclusions, recovery claims, residual-risk notes, or closure recommendations.
